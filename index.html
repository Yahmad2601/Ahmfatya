who are we?

In Nigeria, not only is the cost of electricty a burden, the supply of electricity is unpredictable, unreliable and in many cases-unavailable! This makes alternative energy solutions such as solar energy vital to our lives and critical for our economic development.
Ahmfatya Concept Ltd. is a registered Limited Liability Company in Nigeria, RC No.483919 in june 2003, with its head office at Suite C2, Amma Plaza, Oroago Crescent, Garki 2, Abuja. We offer various services in Electrical Engineering, Solar Home Systems, Solar Mini Grid, Inverter Systems, Energy Audit, Solar Street Lights, Power Management and Rural Electrification at competitive prices, best quality and within the requested time frame.

why choose us?

For homes, offices and businness organizations looking for a cheaper and clean additional source of energy that can be regulated as opposed to normal electricity. Ahmfatya Concept Ltd. is the preferred energy company, because of our area experience and vast knowledge in solar policies, country's regulations, utility company restrictions and building codes specific to your area. We offer fair and honest upfront pricing, provide an accurate energy forecast and also take time to show you the calculations.
We offer post-installation support with little or no extra costs when servicing your systems. In short, we are a reputable energy company with the required policies and certifications which makes us less likely to disappear.
